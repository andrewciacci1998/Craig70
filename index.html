The Man, The Legend, The General Contractor

For decades, Craig Lucio has been building beautiful things in the South Bay — homes, relationships, and an absolutely legendary reputation for good times. Based out of Redondo Beach, this general contractor has spent a lifetime turning blueprints into dream homes and weekends into unforgettable stories.

Now it's time for the man who built it all to kick back in the middle of the Atlantic, surrounded by the people he loves. Golf clubs? Packed. Surfboard? Waxed. Hawaiian shirt? Obviously. Rolling papers? ...no comment.

"Some guys retire and take up gardening. Craig's taking his 70th birthday to a volcanic island in the middle of the ocean. That's the kind of energy we're celebrating."

Travel Essentials

Everything you need to get from Redondo to the Azores

✈️

Getting There

Fly into Ponta Delgada (PDL) on Sao Miguel Island. Direct flights from Boston, New York, Toronto, and Lisbon. Budget carriers like Azores Airlines and SATA offer great deals.

🏨

Accommodation

We've reserved a block of rooms at an oceanfront resort in Ponta Delgada. Details will be shared with your RSVP confirmation.

🛂

Passport & Visa

The Azores are part of Portugal (EU). US & Canadian citizens need a valid passport (no visa for stays under 90 days). Make sure yours doesn't expire within 6 months of travel.

💶

Currency & Tips

Currency is the Euro. Credit cards widely accepted. Tipping 5-10% is customary. ATMs available throughout Ponta Delgada.

🧳

What to Pack

Layers! Azores weather can shift. Bring swimwear, golf attire, a light rain jacket, comfortable walking shoes, and your best Hawaiian shirt. Leave the "herbs" at home — Craig has it covered.

🕐

Time Zone

Azores Time (AZOT) is UTC-1, which is 4 hours ahead of US Eastern / 1 hour behind Lisbon. You'll gain a few hours of paradise!
